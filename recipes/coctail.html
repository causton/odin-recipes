Special Cocktail

A very refreshing rum cocktail with a watermelon twist. All you need are light rum, lime juice, simple syrup, and watermelon puree. This is a house cocktail of the R Bar in New Orleans.

  • Â½ cup chopped seedless watermelon, or as needed
  • ice
  • 3 fluid ounces light rum
  • 1 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1 fluid ounce simple syrup
  1. Blend watermelon in a blender or food processor until smooth
  2. Fill a shaker with ice. Pour 4 fluid ounces watermelon puree, light rum, lime juice, and simple syrup into shaker; cover and shake. Pour cocktail and ice into a pint glass and garnish with lime wedges
